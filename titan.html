TITAN
unity
school project
Titan is a contemplative experience set in an ethereal desert, you play as this almost abstract character picking up fragments of memories to bring back life in your surroundings.

The whole experience is about acceptance of death, from your loved ones to your own death. here are different steps of the visual developpement, leaving the referenced shape to a more personnal and abstract one.
