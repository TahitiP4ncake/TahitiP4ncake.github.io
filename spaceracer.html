SPACE RACER
unity
school project
Space racer is a local multiplayer racing game I'm developing with two classmates. The race takes place on an alien planet human found and each player need to reach the other side of the planet to plant their flag first. To do so they can use their car's ability and jump from one car to another.

I'm doing modeling, animations and gameplay programing

We're taking part of Microsoft's Imagine Cup contest
